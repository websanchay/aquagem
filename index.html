    About Us

    We, at Creatives, as the name suggest is looking to break the monotony of your home, as person we all tend to imagine of a dream home, things that should surround us, things that relax our mind, an atmosphere of our like, all this but not as our neighbour have it ,.

    it should be UNIQUE.... IT SHOULD BE CREATIVE... that is who we are..

    We are committed to provide high quality and high profiled service. With the help of experience that we have gained through our working with some of the renowned name in the field of architecture, interior designing, landscaping, we intend to create a home of your choice & we stand for a total dedication to quality in all that we do.
